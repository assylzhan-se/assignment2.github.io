Tribute to Mark Zuckerberg

About Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg (1984) is an American computer programmer and entrepreneur, best known as the co-founder of Facebook. He launched Facebook in 2004 while studying at Harvard, and it quickly grew into the world's largest social media platform. Zuckerberg has been a key figure in the technology industry, shaping how billions of people connect and communicate.

Important Events

  1. 1984 - Born in White Plains, New York
  2. 2004 - Founded Facebook from his Harvard dorm room
  3. 2012 - Facebook went public (IPO)
  4. 2015 - Launched Internet.org to provide global internet access
  5. 2021 - Rebranded Facebook's parent company to Meta

Major Contributions

  • Creation of Facebook (Meta Platforms)
  • Promoting global connectivity projects
  • Developing virtual and augmented reality technologies
  • Philanthropic initiatives through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative
